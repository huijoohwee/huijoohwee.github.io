# Hackamap: The PMF Map

## **One-liner elevator pitch**  
Hackamap makes product-market fit visible in real time — turning every customer problem into a live map that shows founders exactly where the money is still unclaimed.


## **Short deck-style blurb**

In just 3 hours we’ll show you a free, open-source tool that finally makes product-market fit visible.

- **Live Interactive Canvas**: D3 knowledge graph that instantly connects what people are desperately asking for with every solution already being built.  
- **Gap Score**: One number that flags the biggest unmet needs before anyone else sees them.  
- **Opportunity Finder**: Drag any problem node and watch the whitespace light up — that empty space is your next billion-dollar idea.

## **Why it matters:**  
Hackamap turns noisy demand signals into a single, always-up-to-date opportunity map. 
Founders spot the next big thing in minutes. 
Investors see exactly where the market is still hungry.  

No more guessing. Just clarity.
